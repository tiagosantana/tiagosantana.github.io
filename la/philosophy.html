PHILOSOPHY


My name is Leonel Alexandre and I am the Managing Director and Active Shareholder of 1soccerdream Limited I am adept in scouting undiscovered sportsmen – my target is to support them, so that they evolve their hidden potential to be continuously a winner.Talent Scouting is an important success factor for the future business in every sector – even more in times of demographic gap, where seeking and finding young talents is important to be competitive. This is true for the economy, but especially in sports. My focus is not only to examine the results of the athletes, it is more about merging the total picture that makes a player on the field: technology and teamwork, basic health and social behavior. My scouting experience also includes the ability to recognize the strengths and weaknesses of the opponent, analysing them and giving recommendations based on the analysis. I am equipped with selected international contacts and cooperation partners, dedicated to the teaching of football players at German and international clubs. Another focus of my work is the contact with talented players worldwide.
